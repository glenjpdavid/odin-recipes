Lasagna

Home

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat, cheese, and tomato sauce. It's a hearty meal that is perfect for family gatherings or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • Sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic in a Dutch oven over medium heat until well browned
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water. Season with sugar, 2 tablespoons parsley, basil, 1 teaspoon salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and pepper. Simmer, covered, for about 1.5 hours, stirring occasionally
  4. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8-10 minutes. Drain noodles, and rinse with cold water
  5. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, remaining 2 tablespoons parley, and 1/2 teaspoon salt
  6. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C)
  7. Assemble lasagna
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes
  9. Rest for 15 minutes before serving
