Syrup

Description

Syrup is a sweet liquid made from sugar and water, often flavored with maple or other natural flavors. It's commonly used as a topping for pancakes, waffles, and desserts.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup water

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Combine sugar and water in a medium saucepan over medium heat
  3. Bring to a boil, stirring, until sugar has dissolved. Allow to cool
  4. Pour into a clean jar and seal with a tight-fitting lid; store in the refrigerator for up to one month
