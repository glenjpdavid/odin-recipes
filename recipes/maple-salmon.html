Maple Salmon

Description

Maple Salmon is baked salmon marinaded in a Maple sauce, hence Maple Salmon. It is a delicious and healthy meal to have for lunch.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pound salmon

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Stir maple syrup, soy sauce, garlic, garlic salt, and pepper together in small bowl
  3. Cut salmon into 4 equal-sized fillets
  4. Place in a shallow glass baking dish and coat with maple syrup mixture
  5. Cover the dish and marinate salmon in the refrigerator for 30 minutes, turning once halfway
  6. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F
  7. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake salmon uncovered for 20 minutes
