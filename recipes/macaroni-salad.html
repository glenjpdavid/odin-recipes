Macaroni Salad

Description

Macaroni salad is a staple dish in the United States, and specifically Hawaii. Its main ingrediants are elbow macaroni and mayonnaise. It is usually served as a side dish in Hawaiian plate lucnhes and a side in Thanksgiving dinners.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups uncooked elbow macaroni
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup distilled white vinegar
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons prepared yellow mustard
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 stalks celery, chopped
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, seeded and chopped

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Bring large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
  3. Cook elbow macaroni in the boiling water, stirring occassionally, for 8 minutes
  4. Rinse under cold water and drain
  5. Mix mayonnaise, sugar, vinegar, mustard, salt, pepper, and macaroni together in a large bowl.
  6. Stir in celery, onion, and green pepper
  7. Refrigerate salad for at least 4 hours before serving, but preferably overnight.
