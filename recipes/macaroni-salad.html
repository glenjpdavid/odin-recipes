Macaroni Salad

Description

Macaroni salad is a staple dish in the United States, and specifically Hawaii. Its main ingrediants are elbow macaroni and mayonnaise. It is usually served as a side dish in Hawaiian plate lucnhes and a side in Thanksgiving dinners.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni Noodles
  • Creamy blend of Mayonnaise and Yellow Mustard
  • Sugar and Vinegar
  • Salt and Ground Black Pepper
  • Celery, Onion, Bell Pepper
