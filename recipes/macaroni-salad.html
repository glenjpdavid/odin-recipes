Macaroni Salad

Description

Macaroni salad is a staple dish in the United States, and specifically Hawaii. Its main ingrediants are elbow macaroni and mayonnaise. It is usually served as a side dish in Hawaiian plate lucnhes and a side in Thanksgiving dinners.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni Noodles
  • Creamy blend of Mayonnaise and Yellow Mustard
  • Sugar and Vinegar
  • Salt and Ground Black Pepper
  • Celery, Onion, Bell Pepper

Steps

  1. Cook macaroni in salted water
  2. Mix mayonnaise, sugar, vinergar, mustard, and seasonings together
  3. Stir in the macaroni and remaining ingredients.
  4. Chill macaroni salad in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours before serving, but preferably over night
