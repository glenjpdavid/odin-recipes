Corn on the Cob

Description

A simple and easy recipe for sweet corn on the cob. It is literally just boiling corn in sugar water.

Ingredients

    2 tablespoons white sugar 1 tablespoon lemon juice 6 ears corn on the cob, husks and silk removed

Steps

  1. Fill a large pot about 3/4 full of water and bring to a boil
  2. Stir in sugar and lemon juice until sugar dissolved
  3. Place ears of corn into boiling water, cover the pot, turn off the heat
  4. Let corn cook in the hot water until tender, about 10 minutes
